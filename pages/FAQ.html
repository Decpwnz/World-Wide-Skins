How to buy items on World Wide Skins

You can use the “Buy Now” button to complete the checkout process immediately.
Please note that when purchasing an item, you will only be able to use one type of currency. It is not possible to combine multiple currencies to purchase a single item.

Do I have to be 18+ to use World Wide Skins?

Yes, World Wide Skins is age restricted. You must be 18 years of age or older to use our services. The company reserves the right to ask for proof of age from any customer and customer accounts may be suspended until satisfactory proof of age is provided.

What are the payment methods?

The only one payment method is through Paypal service.

Common Payment Issues

PayPal Payments cannot be made if you are on a VPN or using a mobile device.
Every customer has a limit when adding funds with PayPal. To raise that limit, you will need to go through the whitelisting process.
